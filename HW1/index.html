How I See The World

I have seen the world take many forms. I don't think it has ever been a place that stays the same. It bends and shapes itself to it surroundings, whether that be a change in climate, a cultural shift, a political one, the world is always evolving with and bending to the will the people that inhabit it.

It would be nice to see the world take a kinder turn. I've stopped watching the news, stopped discussing politics, stopped wondering when things will get better. If there is any hope for the Earth, it has to start with the individual. That's why I try my best every day to improve myself and love the people I hold close to me as much as I can. Life is fleeting, but that doesn't mean it has to be a bad or scary thing. Any amount of time is a gift here, even when the world is burning, there is still plenty of beauty to be seen in the fire.
